Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Melodo ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application ("Melodo", or the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our App, including:

Contact Information

  • Name: Used to personalize your experience and for third-party advertising.
  • Email Address: Used for account functionality and authentication. This may be used for tracking purposes to provide personalized services.

Financial Information

  • Payment Information: Collected when you make purchases within the App, including subscriptions. This information is processed by our payment processors and linked to your user identity to provide access to premium features.

Location Information

  • Precise Location: Used to provide location-specific functionality such as finding nearby concerts. This information may be used for tracking purposes to provide relevant recommendations.
  • Coarse Location: Used for general regional functionality and recommendations. This information may also be used for tracking purposes.

User Content

  • Photos or Videos: Collected when you upload content related to your vinyl collection or share content with friends. This content is linked to your user identity.

Identifiers

  • User ID: Used to identify your account and link your activities within the App. This is linked to your identity to provide personalized features.

Usage Data

  • Product Interaction: Information about how you interact with the App, including album reviews, ratings, and collection management. This data is linked to your identity and may be used for tracking purposes to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Personalize your experience with relevant recommendations
  • Connect you with other users based on shared interests
  • Notify you about nearby concerts and music events
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Deliver targeted advertisements (for non-subscribers)
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, and customer service.
  • Advertising Partners: Third parties who help us deliver relevant advertisements to non-subscriber users.
  • Music Service Partners: To access music data and recommendations (like album details from Spotify).
  • Other Users: Information you choose to share with other users through the App's social features.
  • Legal Requirements: When required to comply with applicable laws or legal processes.

Your Choices

You can control your information in several ways:

  • Account Settings: Update or correct your personal information.
  • Privacy Settings: Control what information is visible to other users.
  • Location Permissions: Enable or disable location services through your device settings.
  • Subscription: Upgrade to a premium subscription to remove advertisements.
  • Marketing Communications: Opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information, except where necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect our legitimate business interests.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission or storage system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@melodoapp.com.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have certain rights regarding their personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). This includes the right to know what personal information we collect, disclose, or sell, and the right to delete personal information in certain circumstances.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
